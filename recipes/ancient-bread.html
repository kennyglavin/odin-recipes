Ancient Bread Recipe

Description

This is a flavorful and easy-to-make recipe that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

With fresh ingredients and simple steps, this dish is sure to impress everyone at the table

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together
  2. Your dough should have sort of a play dough consistency There is really no need to knead, just homogenize the ingredients
  3. Let your dough sit in a covered bowl until it roughly doubled in size
  4. Done your dough is ready to bake.
